commission guidelines

General Commission Information

  1. Every commission is defined by the time it takes to work on a request, not the exclusivity of the artwork. Every commission is an explicit exchange of image ownership.
  2. I post WIP screencaps and finished works on my SNS. Private commissions will cost extra as I depend on my artwork to advertise my business.
  3. Do not print my work for profit unless permitted. Commissioners may use their purchases for personal objects like t-shirts and stickers so long as it's not commercial.
  4. Terms of service can change at any time. Slot holders will be informed of new terms presented at time of occupation.
  5. All payments (USD) are taken through Ko-fi unless otherwise specified.
  6. TAT for each piece will vary from a week to months, so name deadlines upon discussion.
  7. I accept price negotiations.

Before you order...

  1. I am a character artist! Please play into my strengths.
  2. Have a reference sheet or image at the ready.
  3. It helps to tell me what you are using the design or art for during the composition stage.

Artist FAQ

What tools? Clip Studio Paint and Gimp. Wacom Tablet, DTZ-1200W LCD.

Can I use your existing artwork as a profile picture or graphic?
Fanarts, yes. Some of my original artworks are commissioned pieces, so I'd advise against using those if you haven't asked the image owners for their permissions.

Request Guidelines

* All skintones and gender identities are welcome.

I specialize in character design, humanoids, and mammal anthros/regular animals. My best compositions are logo, sticker, or charm designs.

I sometimes make exceptions for vehicles/machinery, and other types of anthropomorphics. I also offer both muted NSFW with a specialty in pin-up style artwork and some other types of pornography, but please don't order before e-mailing me privately about details.

I decline reference sheets and real people. Asking for any depictions of underage abuse result in a blacklist position. I may reject a commission without explanation.

Payments & Refunds

Payment upfront, or talk to me about a two payment arrangement. For two-payments, 50% upfront and 50% after delivery.

Refunds are not always guaranteed, and a piece [after composition stage] is invalid for refund.

physical goods

All prints, stickers, 3d printed products are home-manufactured and secured in salvaged packaging (whatever plastic, envelopes, and absorbants I get my hands on). My brand is found with self-sustainability as the model and third party support kept minimal, and I encourage my backers to do follow suit for a kinder, stable community.

Being situated in the US, please brush up on the list of countries our postal service is restricted to send to. A refund is issued in any instance where a supporter's country is sanctioned for whatever reason.
