General Commission Information

  1. You will be paying for my time, not the exclusivity of the art. After payment you will be the image's new owner, but I post finished commissioned works on my SNS to keep art a thriving hobby of mine.
  2. Do not print my work for profit. Commissioners may use their purchases for personal objects like t-shirts and stickers so long as it's not commercial.
  3. Terms of service can change at any time. Occupied slots will be informed of any new terms.
  4. All monetary payments (USD) are taken through Ko-fi unless otherwise specified. I'm refusing to use Paypal for the time being.
  5. TAT for each piece will vary from a week to months, so name deadlines upon discussion.
  6. Negotiate prices if you need to. Final price must be agreeable between both parties
  7. High degree violations (listed below) will put you on a public blacklist.

Request Guidelines

* All skintones and gender identities are welcome.

I specialize in character design, humanoids, and mammal anthros/regular animals. My best compositions are logo, sticker, or charm designs.

I sometimes make exceptions for vehicles/machinery, musician/band fanart, other types of anthropomorphics, slapstick violence, and light raunchy subjects. I can also make a mean CD cover.

I decline reference sheets, real people, and anything pornographic, bloody, or NSFW in that nature. asking for any depictions of child abuse result in a blacklist position.

Before you order...

  1. I take full payments after sketch/composition stage.
  2. If after specified deadline I don't respond, please be patient with me and just keep asking. I make efforts to reply and I manage multiple E-mail inboxes.
  3. I am a character artist! Please play into my strengths.
  4. Have a reference sheet or image at the ready. I'm not doing character design from scratch.
  5. It helps to tell me what you are using the design or art for during the composition stage.
