General Commission Information

  1. Every commission is defined by the time it takes to work on a request, not the exclusivity of the artwork. Every commission is an explicit exchange of image ownership. I post finished commissioned works on my SNS to keep art a thriving hobby of mine.
  2. Do not print my work for profit unless permitted. Commissioners may use their purchases for personal objects like t-shirts and stickers so long as it's not commercial.
  3. Terms of service can change at any time. Slot holders will be informed of new terms presented at time of occupation.
  4. All payments (USD) are taken through Ko-fi unless otherwise specified.
  5. TAT for each piece will vary from a week to months, so name deadlines upon discussion.
  6. I accept price negotiations.

Request Guidelines

* All skintones and gender identities are welcome.

I specialize in character design, humanoids, and mammal anthros/regular animals. My best compositions are logo, sticker, or charm designs.

I sometimes make exceptions for vehicles/machinery, and other types of anthropomorphics. I accept both muted NSFW with a specialty in pin-up style artwork and some other types of pornography (ask for details).

I decline reference sheets and real people. Asking for any depictions of child abuse result in a blacklist position.

Before you order...

  1. I take full payments after sketch/composition stage.
  2. If after specified deadline I don't respond, please be patient with me and just keep asking. I make efforts to reply and I manage multiple E-mail inboxes.
  3. I am a character artist! Please play into my strengths.
  4. Have a reference sheet or image at the ready.
  5. It helps to tell me what you are using the design or art for during the composition stage.
