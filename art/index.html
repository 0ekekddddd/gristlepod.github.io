  • Terms of service may change at any time. Slot holders will be informed of updated terms during queue.
  • All payments (USD) are taken through Ko-fi unless otherwise specified.
  • TAT for each piece will vary from a week to months, so put down deadlines upon discussion.
  • I accept price negotiations and offer alternatives to rejected requests.

Physical Goods

All prints, stickers, 3d printed products are home-manufactured and secured in salvaged packaging (recyclable plastic, envelopes, and absorbants). My brand is found with self-sustainability as the model and third party support kept minimal, and I encourage my backers to follow suit for a kinder, stable community.

Being situated in the US, international buyers are asked to brush up on the list of countries our postal service is restricted to send to. A refund will be issued to all addresses subject.
